Rationale for project

The rationale comes from the school's application form, but you can edit it and add your own analysis.

You could include information about:

  • how governance will work between the school and the trust
  • who will be the head of the school and how will the school be managed
  • if the school has any feeder links that would be important for the HTB to know about

You can enter up to 500 characters
